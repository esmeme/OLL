Open Educational Resource

Learning to Learn "Korean"

Welcome to the final project of the "Open Language Learning" course in the Master's degree in Information and Communication Technologies in Language Teaching and Processing!

This website is the result of our collaborative work in creating a NOOC (Nano Open Online Course) dedicated to learning the Hangul writing system.

Just like in the game of Tetris, where each piece fits perfectly into its place, our Hangul course aims to provide students with a structured and satisfying learning experience.


Each piece of knowledge is like a Tetris block, and our goal is to teach how to fit the pieces together more effectively to understand and master the Korean language in future learning endeavors.

This project has been a great opportunity to develop teaching-learning skills beyond the specific content. In addition to providing knowledge about Hangul, we have explored the reasoning behind the creation of the letters and how this influences the learning process.

The project consists of two main parts:

  • an open digital educational resource (OER) for teaching and learning Hangul, which will be shared through this open online platform,
  • and a document that theoretically supports the design of this resource. Thank you for visiting us, and we hope you enjoy learning with us! Don't forget to leave us your feedback on how we can improve.
